# Sales-Performance-Analysis-with-Excel

## 🔹 Project Overview

Analyzed sales dataset covering regions, product categories, discounts, and profitability

Built an interactive dashboard to monitor sales performance and profit trends

Focused on regional performance, category insights, and pricing impact

Delivered insights to support strategic business decision-making

## 🔹 Tools & Technologies Used

SQL → Data extraction and analysis

Microsoft Excel → Data cleaning and preprocessing

Power BI → Dashboard creation and visualization

## 🔹 Key Features

Regional analysis of sales, profit, and profit margins

Product category performance evaluation

Discount vs sales regression analysis

Identification of top-performing and low-performing segments

Interactive dashboard with key KPIs

## 🔹 Data Analysis Performed

Analyzed sales and profit distribution across regions

Evaluated product category performance in terms of sales and margin

Conducted regression analysis to study impact of discounts on sales

Compared profitability across different business segments

Identified high-performing and underperforming regions and categories

## 🔹 Dashboard Insights

### Regional Performance

East region leads in sales, profit, and profit margin

North region shows high sales but lower profitability

### Category Analysis

Clothing generates highest sales and profit

Electronics has the highest profit margin

Grocery and Sports underperform

### Pricing Insights

Discounts have minimal impact on sales (very low correlation)

## 🔹 Key Business Insights

Strong performance concentrated in East region

Profitability issues in North region despite good sales

High dependence on Clothing category for revenue

Discounts are not an effective driver of sales

Low-performing categories require strategic attention

## 🔹 Business Recommendations

Focus growth strategies on high-performing regions (East)

Improve profitability in North region through pricing optimization

Promote high-margin categories like Electronics

Re-evaluate low-performing categories (Grocery, Sports)

Avoid excessive discounting and focus on value-based pricing

## 🔹 Project Outcome

Identified key drivers of sales and profitability

Enabled data-driven regional and product strategy decisions

Highlighted inefficiencies in pricing and discount strategies

Built a dashboard for continuous performance monitoring
